How it began

The Story Behind
This Website

Every great project has an origin. Ours started not in a boardroom, but on a trail — or rather, before one.

The Spark

A student discovers the Western Ghats

Like thousands of young Maharashtrians, our founder fell in love with trekking — the forts, the mist, the waterfalls that appear only in monsoon. Every weekend became an adventure waiting to happen.

The Problem

2 hours wasted — for a single trek

Planning should be simple. But every search led to scattered blogs, outdated videos, half-baked Reddit threads and contradicting timings. By the time the trail was planned, the excitement was drained.

The Frustration

"Why isn't there one place for all of this?"

The question that changed everything. Waterfall heights, trek difficulty, best seasons, entry timings, parking, nearby villages — all information that exists, but never together in one place.

The Initiative

A student decides to build the solution

Armed with curiosity, code, and a backpack full of trail miles, the decision was made — build the resource that every Maharashtra trekker deserves. Not a startup. Not a business. Just a passion project for the community.

Today

MaharashtraTreks is live 🎉

Treks, waterfalls, gear rentals, difficulty ratings, best seasons and more — all in one place, built with love for everyone who hears the mountains calling.
