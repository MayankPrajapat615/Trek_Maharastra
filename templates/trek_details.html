Join our experienced guides for an unforgettable adventure.


Essential Gear

  • • Sturdy trekking shoes with good grip
  • • 3-4 liters of water
  • • Energy bars, dry fruits & glucose
  • • Rain jacket & poncho (monsoon)
  • • Sunscreen (SPF 50+) and cap
  • • First aid kit & personal medicines
  • • Headlamp/torch with extra batteries
  • • Trekking pole (recommended)

⚠️ Safety First

  • • Never trek alone - minimum group of 3
  • • Check weather forecast 24hrs before
  • • Inform family/friends about your route
  • • Stay on marked trails - don't explore alone
  • • Avoid trekking during heavy rain/storms
  • • Carry fully charged phone + power bank
  • • Know basic first aid & emergency contacts

⏰ Best Time to Start

🌅 Sunrise Trek:

Start at 5:00 AM - Reach summit by 7:30 AM

☀️ Day Trek:

Start at 7:00 AM - Complete by 4:00 PM

💡 Avoid starting after 2 PM to ensure safe descent before dark

Fitness Requirements

This trek requires moderate to good fitness. Prepare with:

  • • 30-min cardio, 5 days/week
  • • Stair climbing practice
  • • 5-7 km walks with a backpack

Start preparation 2-3 weeks before your trek date

💡 Insider Tips

  • • Best Photography: Golden hour (6-8 AM) for stunning fort shots
  • • Food: Try local breakfast at base village - poha & chai are delicious!
  • • Avoid Weekends: Visit on weekdays for a peaceful experience
  • • Carry Cash: Most local vendors don't accept digital payments

🌿 Leave No Trace

  • • Carry back ALL your waste (including peels)
  • • Use designated toilet areas only
  • • Don't pick flowers or disturb wildlife
  • • Stick to trails - avoid creating new paths
  • • Keep noise levels low - respect nature
